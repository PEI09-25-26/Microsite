## MVP - Problem

Real-life Sueca games commonly have issues:

- Manual score keeping
- Mistakes in counting points
- Disagreements about who won a round

> There is no practical, automatic solution for in-person card games!

## MVP - Solution

A mobile app that:

- Uses the smartphone camera
- Recognizes cards and plays
- Automatically tracks points and rounds
- Acts as a referee, identifying cheating individuals

> Technology assists, it does not replace the real game

_In the MVP, we tried to solve Scenario 4's problem._
