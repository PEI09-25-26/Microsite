# Usability Evaluation

## System Usability Scale (SUS)

The **System Usability Scale (SUS)** questionnaire was used to assess the perceived usability of the application.

### Evaluation Procedure

- Participants interacted with the application by performing representative tasks.
- After completing the tasks, users answered the SUS questionnaire.
- The collected responses provided a subjective measure of usability and ease of use.

### Results

- **Number of participants:** 10
- **Average SUS score:** 85.25 / 100

An average SUS score above 80 is generally considered indicative of excellent usability, suggesting a very positive perception of the application.
